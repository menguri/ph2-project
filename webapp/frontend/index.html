Overcooked Human-AI Coordination Study

Welcome

You will play the cooperative cooking game Overcooked with an AI partner.

Your task is to work together to prepare and deliver soup orders as efficiently as possible.

Controls

  • Arrow keys or WASD — Move
  • Space or E — Interact (pick up / place / deliver)

After each game, you'll be asked to fill out a short survey about your experience.

About You

Goal

Cook and deliver onion soup with your AI partner to earn points!

1 Pick up onions
2 Put 3 onions in the pot
3 Wait for soup to cook
4 Pick up a plate, then the soup
5 Deliver to the green serving area

Items

Onion — pick up from the onion pile
Plate — pick up from the plate dispenser
Soup — cooked soup on a plate, ready to deliver

Kitchen Objects

Pot — put 3 onions in, cooking starts automatically
Onion Pile — unlimited onion supply
Plate Dispenser — unlimited plate supply
Serving Area (green) — deliver soup here for points!

Controls

← ↑ → ↓ Arrow keys / WASD — Move & face direction
ACT Space / E — Interact: pick up, put down, or serve

Face the object and press ACT to interact with it.

Tips

You are the BLUE chef
The ORANGE chef is your AI partner

Work together — divide tasks for better scores!
